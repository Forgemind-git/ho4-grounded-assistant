Northwind Trading Co. — Expense Policy
======================================

EXPENSE CLAIMS
--------------
Expense claims must be submitted within 30 days of the expense date.
Receipts are required for all claims over $25.
Claims over $500 require manager pre-approval before the expense is incurred.
Mileage is reimbursed at $0.65 per kilometre for approved business travel.
International travel expenses require VP-level approval regardless of amount.

APPROVED EXPENSE CATEGORIES
---------------------------
Approved categories: meals, accommodation, transport, office supplies, and training materials.

NON-REIMBURSABLE EXPENSES
-------------------------
The following are not reimbursable: gym memberships, personal subscriptions,
traffic fines or penalties, and personal entertainment.

ENTERTAINMENT EXPENSES
----------------------
Client dinners and team events are reimbursable but require a list of attendees and a
short note on the business purpose. Alcohol is reimbursable only at client events, capped
at $30 per person.
