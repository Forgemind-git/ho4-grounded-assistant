Test Questions — HR Policy Assistant
Ask these inside your Claude Project to check it is grounded correctly.

1. How many days of annual leave do I get?
   Expect: 20 days, citing the Annual Leave section.

2. Do I need a receipt for a $20 lunch?
   Expect: No — receipts are only required over $25 (Expense Claims section).

3. What happens if I'm off sick for more than 3 days?
   Expect: A medical certificate is required (Sick Leave section).

4. Do I need approval before a $600 business expense?
   Expect: Yes — claims over $500 require manager pre-approval.

5. How much is the work-from-home internet allowance?
   Expect: "I don't have that information in my knowledge base..." (not in the docs).
