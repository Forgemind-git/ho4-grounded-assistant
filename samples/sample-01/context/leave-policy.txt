Northwind Trading Co. — Leave Policy
====================================

ANNUAL LEAVE
------------
Employees are entitled to 20 days of paid annual leave per calendar year.
Leave must be requested at least 5 business days in advance via the HR portal.
Unused leave can be carried forward up to 5 days into the next calendar year, and
must be used by 31 March or it is forfeited.
Leave taken beyond the entitlement is treated as unpaid leave and must be approved
by your manager in advance.

SICK LEAVE
----------
Employees are entitled to 10 days of paid sick leave per year.
A medical certificate is required for any sick leave exceeding 3 consecutive days.
Sick leave does not carry forward to the next year.
Notify your manager before 9:30am on the first day of absence.

OTHER LEAVE TYPES
-----------------
Parental leave: 12 weeks paid for the primary carer, 2 weeks paid for the secondary carer.
Compassionate leave: up to 5 paid days for the death of an immediate family member.
Study leave: up to 3 paid days per year for pre-approved professional courses or exams.
