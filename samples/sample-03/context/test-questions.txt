Test Questions — Team Onboarding Buddy
Ask these inside your Claude Project to check it is grounded correctly.

1. How do I set up my local environment?
   Expect: The numbered steps from the Setup Runbook.

2. Who do I contact for a design review?
   Expect: Marcus Lee, the Tech Lead (Team Wiki).

3. What time is the daily standup and where?
   Expect: 9:45am on Zoom (Team Wiki).

4. How do I join the on-call rotation?
   Expect: Message @oncall-admin in Slack (Tools Guide).

5. What's the company holiday schedule?
   Expect: "I don't have that information in my knowledge base..." (not in the docs).
