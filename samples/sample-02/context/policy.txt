Acme SaaS — User Documentation
================================

PASSWORD & SECURITY
-------------------
To reset your password:
1. Go to Settings > Security > Change Password.
2. Enter your current password.
3. Enter your new password twice to confirm.
4. Click Save Changes.

Password requirements:
- Minimum 12 characters
- At least one uppercase letter
- At least one number
- Special characters are allowed but not required

If you have forgotten your current password, click "Forgot Password" on the login page.
A reset link will be emailed to your registered address and expires in 60 minutes.

API INTEGRATION
---------------
To connect via API:
1. Go to Settings > Integrations > API Keys.
2. Click Generate New Key.
3. Copy the key immediately — it is shown only once and cannot be retrieved again.
4. Store it securely (e.g. in an environment variable or secrets manager).

API keys can be revoked at any time from the same page.
Rate limit: 1,000 requests per minute per API key.
Authentication: pass the key as a Bearer token in the Authorization header.

DATA EXPORT
-----------
To export your data:
1. Go to Reports > Export.
2. Choose the date range and report type.
3. Select the output format: CSV or PDF.

Export processing times:
- Up to 10,000 rows: instant download (typically under 30 seconds).
- Over 10,000 rows: processed asynchronously and sent to your account email within 2 hours.

Exports are retained for 7 days in the Downloads section of your account.

BILLING
-------
Your subscription renews automatically on the same date each month.
To cancel, go to Settings > Billing > Cancel Subscription.
Cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing period; no refunds for partial months.
To download invoices, go to Settings > Billing > Invoice History.
