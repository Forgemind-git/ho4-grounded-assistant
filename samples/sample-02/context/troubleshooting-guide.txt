Brightdesk — Troubleshooting Guide
==================================

ERROR: "Reset link expired"
Cause: Password reset links expire 60 minutes after they are sent.
Fix: Click "Forgot Password" again to get a fresh link.

ERROR: "Export failed"
Cause: Usually a date range with no data, or a very large export that timed out.
Fix: Narrow the date range. Exports over 10,000 rows are emailed instead of downloaded.

ISSUE: New tickets not appearing
Cause: The connected inbox token has expired.
Fix: Go to Settings > Channels > Email and click Reconnect.

ESCALATION
----------
If a problem is not listed here, contact support@brightdesk.example.com.
Include your account email and a screenshot. Support replies within one business day.
