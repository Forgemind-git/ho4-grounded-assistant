Test Questions — Product Support Assistant
Ask these inside your Claude Project to check it is grounded correctly.

1. How do I reset my password?
   Expect: The numbered steps from the Account Management section.

2. How many agents can I have on the Starter plan?
   Expect: Up to 3 agents (FAQ).

3. Why did my export fail?
   Expect: Empty date range or too-large export; narrow the range (Troubleshooting).

4. Can I get my API key back if I lost it?
   Expect: No — revoke and create a new one (FAQ).

5. Does Brightdesk have a mobile app?
   Expect: "I don't have that information in my knowledge base..." (not in the docs).
