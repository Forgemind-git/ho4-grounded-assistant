Brightdesk — Product Manual
===========================

GETTING STARTED
---------------
To create an account, go to app.brightdesk.example.com and click "Sign up".
Brightdesk works in the latest two versions of Chrome, Edge, Firefox, and Safari.

ACCOUNT MANAGEMENT
------------------
To reset your password:
1. Go to Settings > Security > Change Password.
2. Enter your current password, then your new password twice.
3. Click Save Changes.
Password requirements: at least 12 characters, one uppercase letter, and one number.
If you forgot your password, click "Forgot Password" on the login page; the reset link
emails to your registered address and expires in 60 minutes.

KEY FEATURES
------------
Tickets: incoming customer messages become tickets. Assign, tag, and set a priority.
Macros: saved reply templates. Create them in Settings > Macros.
Reports: see ticket volume, response time, and CSAT in the Reports tab.

DATA AND EXPORTS
----------------
To export data, go to Reports > Export and choose CSV or PDF.
Up to 10,000 rows download instantly. Larger exports are emailed to you within 2 hours.
Exports are kept for 7 days in the Downloads section.

BILLING
-------
Your subscription renews automatically on the same date each month.
To cancel, go to Settings > Billing > Cancel Subscription. Cancellation takes effect at the
end of the current billing period; there are no refunds for partial months.
