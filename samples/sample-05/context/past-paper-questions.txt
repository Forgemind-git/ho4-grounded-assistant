ML101 — Past Paper Questions
============================

EXAM YEAR: 2023
---------------
Question 1: Explain the difference between supervised and unsupervised learning. [6 marks]
Model Answer: Supervised learning trains on labelled data to map inputs to known outputs
(e.g. spam detection). Unsupervised learning finds structure in unlabelled data
(e.g. customer segmentation). Key difference: labels are present vs absent.

Question 2: Describe the four steps of training a neural network. [8 marks]
Model Answer: Forward pass, loss calculation, backpropagation, weight update (gradient
descent). See Chapter 2.

EXAM YEAR: 2022
---------------
Question 1: Define precision and recall and explain when each matters. [6 marks]
Model Answer: Precision = TP/(TP+FP), important when false alarms are costly. Recall =
TP/(TP+FN), important when missing a positive is costly.
