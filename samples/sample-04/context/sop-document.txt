Data Protection & Compliance — Standard Operating Procedure (SOP-DPC-001)
=========================================================================
Version 2.3 | Effective: 2024-01-01 | Owner: Data Protection Officer (DPO)

SCOPE
-----
This SOP covers the storage, retention, access, and breach handling of customer
personal data. It applies to all employees and contractors.

DATA STORAGE
------------
Customer data must be stored only in approved EU-region servers:
AWS eu-west-1 (Ireland) and AWS eu-central-1 (Frankfurt).
Any transfer of personal data outside the EU requires a Data Transfer Agreement (DTA)
signed by the DPO before the transfer takes place.
Temporary copies on laptops must be encrypted and deleted within 48 hours of task completion.

DATA RETENTION & DELETION
-------------------------
Personal data must be deleted within 30 days of a customer deletion request.
Deletion requests must be logged in the Data Requests Register within 24 hours of receipt.
Backups containing personal data must also be purged within the 30-day window.

ACCESS CONTROL
--------------
Access to personal data is restricted to employees holding the Data Access role.
The Data Access role is granted by the DPO only, via the IT helpdesk with a business
justification. Access is recertified quarterly and revoked within 24 hours of an
employee's last day.

SECURITY INCIDENTS
------------------
Security incidents involving personal data must be reported to the DPO within 2 hours of
detection, at any time of day. The DPO decides whether the supervisory authority must be
notified (required within 72 hours of a qualifying breach).

AUDIT LOGS
----------
Audit logs must be retained for 7 years in an immutable (write-once) format.
Access to audit logs is restricted to the DPO and the CISO.
