Data Protection & Compliance — Standard Operating Procedure (SOP-DPC-001)
==========================================================================
Version 2.3 | Effective: 2024-01-01 | Owner: Data Protection Officer (DPO)

DATA STORAGE
------------
Customer data must be stored only in approved EU-region servers.
Currently approved data centres: AWS eu-west-1 (Ireland), AWS eu-central-1 (Frankfurt).
Any data transfer outside the EU requires a Data Transfer Agreement (DTA)
signed by the DPO prior to the transfer.
Temporary copies of data on developer laptops must be encrypted at rest and
deleted within 48 hours of the task being complete.

DATA RETENTION & DELETION
--------------------------
Personal data must be deleted within 30 days of receiving a customer deletion request.
Deletion requests must be logged in the Data Requests Register within 24 hours of receipt.
Automated deletion workflows must be verified by the DPO before being put into production.
Backups containing personal data must also be purged within the 30-day window.

AUDIT LOGS
----------
Audit logs must be retained for 7 years in accordance with financial regulation.
Logs must be stored in an immutable format (write-once, cannot be altered).
Access to audit logs is restricted to the DPO and the CISO.

ACCESS CONTROL
--------------
Access to personal data is restricted to employees with a Data Access role.
The Data Access role is granted by the DPO only.
Access requests must be submitted via the IT helpdesk with a business justification.
Access is reviewed and recertified quarterly.
Departing employees must have all data access revoked within 24 hours of their last day.

SECURITY INCIDENTS
------------------
Security incidents involving personal data must be reported to the DPO within 2 hours
of detection, regardless of the time of day.
The DPO will assess whether the incident requires notification to the supervisory authority
(required within 72 hours of the organisation becoming aware of a qualifying breach).
All incidents must be logged in the Incident Register using the standard template.
