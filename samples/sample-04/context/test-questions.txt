Test Questions — Compliance SOP Assistant
Ask these inside your Claude Project to check it is grounded correctly.
In compliance, a wrong answer matters — test the refusal and violation cases carefully.

1. Can I store customer data on a US server?
   Expect: No — only approved EU regions; a DTA signed by the DPO is needed to transfer out.

2. How long do I have to delete data after a deletion request?
   Expect: Within 30 days (Data Retention & Deletion).

3. When must I report a security incident?
   Expect: To the DPO within 2 hours of detection (Security Incidents).

4. How long must audit logs be kept?
   Expect: 7 years, in immutable format (Audit Logs).

5. What is the policy on using customer data for marketing emails?
   Expect: "This is not covered in the current SOP documents. Please escalate to the DPO..."
