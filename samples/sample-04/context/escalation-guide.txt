Data Protection — Escalation Guide
==================================

WHEN TO ESCALATE
----------------
Escalate when: a situation is not covered by SOP-DPC-001, a potential breach is suspected,
or a request would require transferring data outside the EU.

ESCALATION CONTACTS
-------------------
Primary contact: the Data Protection Officer (DPO) — dpo@company.example.com.
Backup contact: the CISO.
For a suspected breach, contact the DPO immediately by phone, then email.
Expected response time for an escalation: within 2 hours during business hours.

ESCALATION PROCEDURE
--------------------
1. Document the situation in the Incident Register.
2. Notify the DPO within the timeframe above.
3. Do not take further action on the data until the DPO provides guidance.
