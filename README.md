# HO4 — Grounded Q&A Assistant

> Hands-on portfolio project · **Week 2** · **Solo** · module M7. Part of the **ForgeMind AI — AI Productivity Essentials** course.

## Goal

**Done when:** Cited answers from a doc set · a before/after demo

## What to ship

The context pack (sources + instructions) + a test-question set + a before(hallucinated)/after(cited) demo.

## Pick a problem statement

Choose **one** of these real use-cases — or bring your own (get it approved first):

1. Your HR inbox keeps getting the same leave and expense questions and staff misquote the rules. Build an assistant grounded only in your HR handbook that answers policy questions and cites the section it came from. Success: a before demo where ungrounded Claude invents a policy and an after demo where it answers correctly with a citation.

2. Support agents at your company waste time digging through manuals mid-chat. Build an assistant grounded only in your product docs, FAQs and release notes that answers 'how do I reset this?' with a cited source. Success: a before/after demo showing the ungrounded answer hallucinating a feature versus the grounded answer citing the real doc.

3. New joiners on your team ask the same setup questions for weeks. Build an onboarding buddy grounded only in your team wiki and runbooks that answers their questions and points to the exact page. Success: a before demo where the model guesses your internal process and an after demo where it answers accurately with a citation.

4. You own a regulated SOP where a wrong answer has real consequences and guessing is unacceptable. Build a compliance assistant grounded only in the approved procedure that answers staff and refuses when the docs do not cover it. Success: a before/after demo proving it stops hallucinating and instead cites the SOP step.

5. You are a student revising from a syllabus and dense readings and want answers you can trust before an exam. Build a study assistant grounded only in those materials that answers questions and cites the source page. Success: a before demo where ungrounded Claude invents a fact and an after demo where it answers with the correct cited page.
